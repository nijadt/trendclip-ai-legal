Privacy Policy – TrendClip AI

Last updated: November 2025

TrendClip AI ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy.
This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

  • OAuth Data: When you connect TikTok, we receive an access token and limited profile information (username and user ID).
  • Video Data: Public video metadata from YouTube (titles, thumbnails, statistics).
  • Usage Logs: Basic analytics for performance monitoring (no personal identifiers).

2. How We Use Information

  • To analyze and repurpose public videos for short-form content.
  • To upload videos to TikTok on your behalf, with your consent.
  • To improve the Service's algorithms and reliability.

3. Data Storage and Retention

We store minimal data required for processing. Tokens are encrypted and automatically expire according to TikTok's policy.
Video files may be temporarily cached and deleted after processing or upload.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or share your personal data.
APIs used: TikTok Open API, YouTube Data API, and Microsoft Graph (OneDrive).
Each provider's data use is governed by its own privacy policy.

5. Security

We apply reasonable technical measures (encryption, HTTPS) to protect your data, but no online service is 100 % secure.

6. Your Rights

You may revoke TikTok OAuth access at any time in your TikTok account settings.
Contact us to request deletion of any retained data.

7. Children's Privacy

TrendClip AI is not intended for children under 13.

8. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Updates will appear on this page with a new "Last updated" date.

9. Contact

Questions? Email privacy@trendclip.ai.
